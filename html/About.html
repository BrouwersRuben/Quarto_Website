About us

Our team name Wild Llama Entertainment was chosen on our first day together.
We came up with this name through a rapper name generator. After we saw that name, we put "Entertainment" behind it, and went with it.

We loved the name, the Wild stand for the innovative and forward-thinking mentality we have.

We like the Llama part of our name, because a Llama symbolises someone or something that can endure harsh environments very well, they have great perseverance, and last but not least. Llama's adapt well to changing conditions.
