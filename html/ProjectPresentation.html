The Game Quarto

We got Quarto as the game we had to develop, it dates back to 1991. This game is variant of the popular game Tick Tack Toe, where you have to form a line with 3 identical pieces.

our game has 16 different pieces, all with 4 different elements. You also have to make a line of 4 pieces with matching elements. But there is a big difference between our game and Tick Tack Toe. This is that with Quarto, you have to pick the piece of your opponent.

Game Rules

The aim of the game is to complete a line with four pieces that are similar at least about one of the four described characteristics (four big pieces, four little, four green, four blue, four circle, four triangle, four with hole or four without hole). The line may be vertical, horizontal or diagonal. The winner is the player who places the fourth piece of the line.
