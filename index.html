Gnome Explorers

We based our game 'Gnome Explorers' of the already existing and successful game 'Dwarf Fortress'.

Dwarf Fortress was officially made by Bay12 in 2006. His original goal was "to create a fantasy world simulator in which it is possible to take part in a rich history, occupying a variety of roles through the course of several games."

We tried to fulfil this as closely as possible, as our game is smaller and as we had less time and manpower to develop, we had to skip out on some of the features that "Dwarf Fortress" does give you, like auto generating maps, a detailed storyline and many many treasures.

Gnome Explorers is a high intensity, easy to play, no story line, heartbeat raising game, with an easter egg...
Make sure to play it and let us know if you find the way to the golden chamber yourself, or perhaps beat some of our high scores!
